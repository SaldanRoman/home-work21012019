3# LABRADOR-RETRIEVER

Labradors have many professions: they are used at customs and in the police, where they are looking for drugs and explosives, for hunting and cattle grazing, moreover, often labradors work as rescuers - in mountain and water rescue services.

2# GERMAN SHEPHERD

This popular breed of dogs was brought out in Germany in 1899. These dogs have a good sense of smell and courage, endurance and hard-working.Importantly, the dog considers only one man to be the owner.

1# BORDER COLLIES

Border Collie is a shepherd dog breed brought out on the border between England and Scotland, where its name is Border, which in English means "the border". Border Collie is recognized as the most intelligent and best dog in the world and the reason for this is their mind, temperament and unsurpassed instinct of the shepherd.
